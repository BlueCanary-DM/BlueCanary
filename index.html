Our services

Blue Canary focusses on assisting small and medium-sized businesses in Belgium to increase their cyber security maturity. We like to offer our past experiences in high-secure and hardened environements without the premium pricetag. Because we believe in a clear and transparant pricing policy we opted for a fixed price packages for small and medium-sized businesses to gain insights in your current cyber security level.

Prior to testing, we setup a physical meeting to discuss your expectations, scope, practicalities and pricing.

  • Cyber Security check-up

    We will assess your overal cyber security maturity by performing vulnerability scans in your network, performing an access control review After the test, you will receive a detailed report on your cyber security maturity measured against other companies and recommendations to improve your resilience against cybersecurity risks

  • Penetration testing

    For a number of days, we will act both as an external attacker and a malicious insider to attempt to penetrate and compromise your internal network and applications. After the test, you will receive a detailed report with identified vulnerabilities, possible attack scenario's and advice on remediation. We also offer penetration testing against specific targets (e.g. a web application)

  • Vulnerability management

    When the unthinkable happens, you want to rely on tested procedures to minimise the damage and recover as soon as possible. We can assist you in setting up incident procedures

  • Social Engineering

    We will setup and launch a phishing attack against your business to create cyber security awareness amongst your employees.
